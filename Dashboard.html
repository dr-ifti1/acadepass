Cardiovascular Drugs

This lecture covers the essential cardiovascular drugs that every nursing professional must know. We'll explore antihypertensives, antianginal drugs, heart failure medications, and anticoagulants. Learn about mechanisms of action, therapeutic uses, side effects, and nursing considerations for each drug class.

Key topics include ACE inhibitors, beta-blockers, calcium channel blockers, diuretics, nitrates, and statins. Understanding these medications is crucial for the NLE exam and your nursing career.
